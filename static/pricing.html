// FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FAQ

What data sources does the platform use?
All intelligence is derived from open-source, publicly available data. Sources include CelesTrak orbital data, NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center, NASA APIs (NEO, DONKI, EONET, EPIC, FIRMS), Launch Library 2, SatNOGS RF monitoring, USGS seismic data, ArXiv academic papers, social media OSINT, and more. No classified information is ingested, processed, or stored. The platform is fully OSINT-based.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. The full dashboard at /app is freely accessible for evaluation. This includes all 16 dashboard tabs, real-time satellite tracking, AI deductions, and space weather monitoring. Paid tiers add API access for programmatic integration, custom hotspot monitoring, the wargame scenario builder, dedicated support, strategic briefings, and enterprise deployment options.
Is the platform ITAR restricted?
No. Echelon Vantage is fully ITAR-free. All intelligence is derived from open sources with no classification restrictions. The platform can be shared across all Five Eyes partners, NATO allies, CSpO members, and coalition partners without export control concerns or foreign disclosure requirements. Australian-owned and operated with no US-origin controlled technology.
Can it be deployed on-premises?
Yes, on the Enterprise tier. Echelon Vantage can be deployed to air-gapped networks, classified enclaves, or on-premises infrastructure. The platform operates with cached data feeds for environments without external network access. Contact sales@echelonvantage.com for deployment architecture discussions and requirements.
Which countries can access the platform?
Echelon Vantage is available to all Five Eyes partner nations (Australia, United States, United Kingdom, Canada, New Zealand) and allied nations. This includes NATO members, CSpO participants (France, Germany), Quad partners (India, Japan), and other allied defence and intelligence organisations. The OSINT-based approach eliminates the access restrictions that limit classified SDA product dissemination.
